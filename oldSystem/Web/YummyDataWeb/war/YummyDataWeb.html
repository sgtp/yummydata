YummyData Endpoint Monitor

This service monitors various attributes of SPARQL endpoints, such as response time, data size, and domain-dependent quality attributes. We summarise the findings in a simplified number called the Sparkle Rating, which is a weighted sum. Due to the inherent difficulty of measuring quality in a general way, this number may not reflect the quality of the endpoints or their data for the purposes of a specific application.

This service was developed at BioHackathon 2012 (wiki) in the YummyData project. Please contact us if you have any comments or questions, or if you want us to add your endpoint or custom metrics: andrea.splendiani (at) iscb.org, jtnystrom (at) gmail.com.
